    okujuga

    When the boy liked the girl, before approaching her would first let his parent know if she was the right girl for him. The parents would aprove of the girl and the decision was made.they carried out "Okugamba Obugyenyi" The boy's family would visit the girls family to ask for the daughter's hand in marriage. It was up to the girl's family to allow the proposal. The girl did not have any say because the elders were always right. So the girls family would decide the bride price of their daughter. They nomally used cows as there bride price, the girl's family would decide on how many cows were to be brought.
